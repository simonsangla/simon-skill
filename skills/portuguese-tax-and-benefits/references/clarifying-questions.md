# Clarifying questions — what to ask before answering

Most Portuguese tax and benefit questions cannot be answered correctly without facts the user has not volunteered. **Before drafting an answer, work through the relevant block below.** If a fact would change the answer materially, ask. If it would change it marginally, state your assumption and flag it in the "Risks" section.

This file is organised by domain. For cross-domain questions, walk through each relevant block.

---

## Universal — ask on every question

1. **Tax residency status** — resident, non-resident, NHR (in window), IFICI beneficiary, just-moved (and exact date of arrival).
2. **Prior PT tax / contribution history** — first time filing IRS in PT? First time registering at IEFP? Years of contribution to Seg Social?
3. **NIF and NISS** — confirmed obtained, both?
4. **Tax year at issue** — current year, prior year (which?), forward-looking?
5. **Household composition** — married / união de facto, dependents, joint vs separate filing if applicable.
6. **Source(s) of income** — list every category (A, B, E, F, G, H) the user has receipts under.

---

## IRS — additional questions

### General
7. Annual gross income, by category, current year (estimate is fine).
8. Withholding already suffered? (Retenção na fonte — visible on Portal das Finanças.)
9. Are e-Fatura invoices fully attributed to the right category?
10. Any foreign-source income? From which country, what nature?
11. Any treaty-relevant facts (dual residency, foreign tax already paid)?

### Categoria B specific
12. Activity opened — when, under which CAE / Article 151.º profession code?
13. Regime simplificado or contabilidade organizada? Turnover trend last 2 years?
14. Clients — Portuguese, foreign, single dominant client (economic dependence risk)?
15. Pluriactividade — any concurrent Categoria A contract?
16. Seg Social independente — declarações trimestrais up to date?
17. VAT — covered by Article 53.º exemption or charging IVA?

### IRS Jovem / IFICI
18. Date of birth.
19. Year first earned Categoria A or B income **as a non-dependent** — this starts the 10-year IRS Jovem window. There is **no education requirement** (the ensino-secundário condition was abolished by OE 2025); do not ask about qualifications as an eligibility gate.
20. Years (if any) the user was a **dependent** in another household's IRS while earning Categoria A/B income — those years do not count toward the 10-year window.
21. Prior years already benefited from IRS Jovem under any version?
22. For IFICI: nature of activity, qualifying R&D / higher-ed / other listed activity?

### Mais-valias / property sale
23. Acquisition date, acquisition price, IMT and IS paid at acquisition, invoiced improvement works.
24. Property use during ownership — habitação própria e permanente, secondary, rental?
25. Intention to reinvest in another primary residence within window?

---

## Property tax — additional questions

26. Property type — urban habitação, urban building-land, rural, mixed?
27. **VPT** as currently registered (Caderneta Predial)?
28. Use of the property — habitação própria e permanente, secondary, rental, vacant?
29. Acquisition price (or future expected price for a planned purchase)?
30. Acquisition date or planned escritura date?

### IMI specifically
31. Município?
32. Family deduction claimed? Dependents living in the property?
33. Any exemption regime already active (HPP low-VPT, ARU, etc.) and on what start date?

### IMT / Imposto do Selo specifically
34. First permanent residence for the buyer? Buyer's age and date of birth?
35. Buyer's tax residency at time of acquisition?
36. Property to be the buyer's habitação própria e permanente?
37. Property in an ARU? Works planned?
38. Purchase via individual or via company?

### AIMI specifically
39. Aggregate VPT of all habitação + terreno-para-construção owned by the user (and spouse if relevant)?
40. Joint AIMI option exercised (couples)?

---

## IEFP / Segurança Social — additional questions

### Status
41. Currently registered as desempregado at IEFP? From which date?
42. Currently receiving subsídio de desemprego? At what monthly amount, from which date, for what computed duration?
43. Reason for unemployment — termination by employer, end of fixed-term, mutual revocation, resignation?
44. Contribution history — months of contribution in last 24 months as TCO? Any independente contributions?
45. Plano pessoal de emprego — current obligations and convocatórias?

### Self-employment intentions
46. Is atividade currently open at Finanças? When opened or closed?
47. Has any ACPE (Apoio à Criação do Próprio Emprego) request been filed? Status?
48. Expected first month of revenue under the new activity?
49. Expected monthly *rendimento relevante* from the activity (services receipts × 0,70, or goods/hotelaria × 0,20) — and how it compares to the user's own monthly subsídio value. That comparison, **not any IAS multiple**, is the subsídio parcial compatibility test (see `iefp-seg-social.md` §3).
50. Recurring vs one-off clients? Single dominant client?

### Compatibility
51. Any unreported income earned during benefit period?
52. Travel abroad planned during benefit period?
53. Any refused job offers from IEFP convocatórias?

### Household
54. Spouse / household income — for any subsídio social or RSI eligibility test?

---

## Cross-domain — ask if scenario involves both tax and benefits

55. **If receiving subsídio de desemprego and considering Categoria B**:
   - Has ACPE been considered?
   - Is the user willing to declare proactively via Segurança Social Direta?
   - What is the *expected* monthly *rendimento relevante*, and is it below the user's monthly subsídio value? (The parcial compatibility test is rendimento relevante < subsídio — there is no IAS-multiple threshold.)
   - Is there a "*emprego conveniente*" offer outstanding from IEFP?

56. **If buying property while unemployed**:
   - Source of funds — declared income, savings, family help, mortgage? Implications for IRS (Categoria G if interest), Imposto do Selo on a gift, AT scrutiny on declared funds vs reported income (manifestações de fortuna risk).

57. **If inheriting property**:
   - Date of death, relationship to deceased, heirs' tax residency, partilha planned, intention to sell or hold?

---

## How to ask

Ask the *fewest* questions that change the answer. If a question is decisive (e.g., residency for any IRS matter), ask it as a single direct question rather than burying it in a list. If multiple questions are needed, group them, explain why you need them, and let the user dump answers in any order.

Do **not** demand all 50+ items above for every question — that is theatre and wastes the user's time. The list is a checklist for the assistant, not a script to recite.
