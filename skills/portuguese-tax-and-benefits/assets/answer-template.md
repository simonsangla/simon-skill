# Answer template — Facts / Analysis / Risks / Actions / Documents / Sources

Use this skeleton for every substantive answer in scope. Section headings are fixed strings — do not rename them. Body content is yours to shape.

---

```
## Facts

[1-3 short paragraphs OR a tight bulleted list. State the user's situation as you understood it (with assumptions called out), plus the *settled* law you will apply. Keep this section narrow — speculation belongs in Analysis.]

## Analysis

[How the law applies to the facts. Distinguish:
 - "The statute / code / diploma says X" — settled
 - "AT's / Seg Social's published guidance reads X" — administrative interpretation
 - "My reading is X because Y" — your interpretation
 - "This depends on a fact you have not stated" — call it out
If a calculation is required, show it step by step. Cite each input value next to where it is used.]

## Risks

[An honest enumeration of what could go wrong. Cover:
 - Compliance risks (missed deadline, undeclared item, requalification of income)
 - Audit / inspection risks (manifestações de fortuna, AT cross-checks)
 - Benefit-loss risks (Seg Social claw-back, IEFP convocatória failure)
 - Verification risks ("I could not confirm value X in this session")
 - Fact-dependency risks (changes in residency, age cut-offs, marital status)
 - Regime-change risks (pending OE amendments, transitional rules)
Be specific. "Tax law is complex" is not a risk.]

## Actions

[Numbered, ordered, deliverable. For each:
 - What to do
 - When (deadline or window)
 - Where (Portal das Finanças path, Segurança Social Direta path, IEFPonline, Loja de Cidadão, Câmara Municipal)
 - Who must do it (the user, the user's TOC / contabilista certificado, a notary)]

## Documents needed

[What to gather before taking the Actions. Use the official document name and the issuing authority. E.g.:
 - Modelo 1 do IMI (AT)
 - Caderneta Predial Urbana (AT, Portal das Finanças → Imóveis)
 - Comprovativo de inscrição no IEFP
 - Declaração de IRS do ano N-1 (AT)
 - Comprovativo de IBAN (banco)]

## Source links

[Bulleted. For each:
 - Title of the page / diploma
 - URL
 - Publication or "consolidado a" date if visible (say "date not displayed" if not)
 - Mark of authority — "primary" (DRE, AT, Seg Social, IEFP, câmara) or "secondary" (CAAD, doctrinal) — primary first.]
```

---

## Usage notes

- **Do not collapse sections.** Even when "Risks" feels light, write at least the verification risk and any fact-dependency risk. Even when "Documents needed" feels obvious, list them — it is the single most actionable part of the answer for the user.
- **Do not paraphrase headings.** Tools and viewers may key on these literal strings.
- **Do not embed a TL;DR before "## Facts".** The structure is the TL;DR; an opener defeats the purpose.
- **Do put a one-line caveat at the very top when verification was incomplete.** E.g., `_Verification status: structure described; current-year thresholds not retrievable in this session — see "Source links" to confirm before acting._`
- **Keep the answer roughly proportionate to the question.** A simple, well-bounded question deserves a tight answer in this format; a complex multi-domain question can run longer.
