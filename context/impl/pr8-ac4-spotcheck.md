# PR8 follow-up — AC4 reference-file date-stamp spot-check

Deferred from PR8 (`context/impl/pr8-triage.md` §5 step 4). Verifies
`cavekit-background-skills.md` **R3 ACC4** against every per-domain reference
file under `skills/portuguese-tax-and-benefits/references/`.

## ACC4 (verbatim)

> Every numeric threshold in the reference files appears next to a publication
> date or a "consolidado a" date, or is explicitly flagged with the phrase
> "date not displayed".

## Files reviewed (full read)

`irs.md` · `property-tax.md` · `iefp-seg-social.md` ·
`self-employed-seg-social.md` · `cross-border.md` · `sources.md`.

## Result — ACC4 HIT

Every annually-revised euro threshold (subsídio floors/ceilings, IAS multiples,
IRS Jovem ceiling, mínimo de existência, AIMI allowances + brackets, IMT
escalão limits, IMT Jovem ceilings) sits under a `2026`/`2025`-tagged
subsection heading, a table column keyed to the year, or a named diploma with
its publication date. `sources.md` carries the bundle-wide refresh log
(2026-05-13) anchoring the diploma set. Treaty dates in `cross-border.md` and
the W-8BEN revision (October 2021) are stamped. Worked-example figures are
hypotheticals, not thresholds. Structural rates and ratios (coefficients, age
limits, day-counts, contribution percentages) carry explicit "verify" hedges
by design — they are not year-keyed thresholds.

## Tightening applied

One outlier: `property-tax.md` §2 — the IMI Familiar per-dependent amounts
`€30 / €70 / €140` carried no temporal anchor or diploma cite. Anchored to
**Art. 112.º-A CIMI** and flagged with the literal "date not displayed" escape
phrase per ACC4. No other drift found; no value changed substance.
